Tour Overview

Embark on an unforgettable journey from Gujarat to the breathtaking landscapes of Himachal Pradesh with our Gujarat to Manali Super Saver Tour. Starting from Ahmedabad, this budget-friendly group tour takes you through Punjab and into the Himalayas, offering a mix of cultural heritage, snowy adventures, and serene valleys. Experience the Golden Temple's spiritual aura, the thrill of snow activities in Solang Valley, and the scenic beauty of places like Kasol and Kullu. Designed for travelers seeking value, this tour includes comfortable accommodations, guided sightseeing, and a dedicated tour manager to ensure a smooth experience.

Day Wise Itinerary

Depart from Ahmedabad by sleeper class train to Amritsar. Enjoy onboard facilities and prepare for the cultural journey ahead.

Visit the Golden Temple for a spiritual experience, explore Jallianwala Bagh Memorial, and witness the Wagah Border ceremony in the evening.

Travel from Amritsar to Manali by bus or taxi, with an overnight stay en route to acclimate to the mountain terrain.

Arrive in Manali, check into hotel, and explore local attractions like Hadimba Temple and Mall Road for shopping and cafes.

Head to Solang Valley for skiing, paragliding, and other snow-based adventures (weather permitting).

Drive through the Atal Tunnel, enjoy Sissu Valley views, and relax in Vashisht's natural hot springs.

Travel to Kasol, settle in, and explore the Parvati Valley's cafes and riverside walks.

Visit Manikaran Sahib Gurudwara and hot springs, then proceed to Kullu for an overnight stay at Swiss Camp.

Depart from Kullu towards Ahmedabad, with stops as needed for rest and meals.

Arrive back in Ahmedabad, concluding the tour with fond memories of the Himalayas.
